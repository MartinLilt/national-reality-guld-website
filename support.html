You're Not Alone with NRG

Every agent that joins with NRG gets the full support and dedication of our team. Whether you are new in business or looking for ways to increase business, we are here to help you grow!

  • On-Board Training

    Getting started with NRG is easy. We’ll guide you through the process and train you how to use all the tools you need to get your business going.

  • Full Administrative Support

    All our office locations offer full administrative support. Whether you need help with answering phones or putting together presentations, we’ve got you covered.

  • Easy Broker Access

    Have a question for the Broker? As an agent at NRG, you will always have easy access to your broker and will never have to navigate this business alone.

  • On-Site Continuing Education

    Every month we offer continuing education classes, right here in our office! Not only will this help knock out some of your CE credits, we get to train together!

  • Accountability Sessions

    Accountability sessions are a great way to help agents stay on track. We dive into what’s working, what’s not, and develop a strategy for your success.

  • Exclusive Training Sessions

    We offer valuable training sessions every month, exclusive to NRG agents! Team up with a broker who invests in your success and provides the training you need to grow!

Full support makes switching to NRG easy. We help you properly prepare by designing personalized signs for listings, order business cards, activate a corporate email, set-up voicemail, agent websites, domain forwarding, and anything else necessary to assure a smooth transition.
