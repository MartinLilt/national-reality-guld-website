Technology & Lead Generation

Are you a real estate professional who’s looking to grow your realty business and get more clients? The National Realty Guild offers some of the best resources available to help you bring in new, reliable clients. Our real estate brokers use advanced technology and the latest tools to help you get your name out in the real estate world and in front of hundreds of new clients all around Minnesota. The professionals at NRG take lead generation seriously and will help you grow your business in every way we can. All of these reasons and more help make the National Realty Guild one of the best real estate brokers in the country. See below for some of the lead generation benefits NRG can offer you.

  • Agent Website

    Build a strong digital presence and watch as your real estate business takes off. We’ll make sure you stand out with a professionally developed agent website.

  • Customer Relationship Manager (CRM)

    Keep track of your real estate clients, leads, appointments, and more with a dedicated Customer Relationship Manager from the National Realty Guild.

  • Enhanced Listing Tools

    Need a way to get more real estate listings? NRG has some of the most powerful and effective listing tools available to help grow your real estate business

  • Single-Property Websites

    Single-property websites from NRG are some of the best resources you can use to build your listings. Our dedicated real estate sites are made to help your listings shine.

  • Automated Prospecting Programs

    Let the advanced technology at NRG go to work for you. These programs ensure that you’re always staying in front of potential real estate clients.

  • Listing Syndication

    Save time and money when you use NRG’s enhanced Listing Syndication tools that make sure your listings are accurately presented on every platform available.

  • Lead Generation Tools

    Lead generation is a vital tool when it comes to real estate careers. NRG provides the latest technology and tools to keep new and dependable leads coming in.

  • Lead Tracking Technology

    With NRG, you can manage and track all of your leads so you can turn them into business. Lead tracking will show you what’s working and what isn’t, helping you optimize your strategies.

  • Contact Us Today

    Whether you’re just becoming a real estate agent or you’re looking to change brokers, our team of real estate professionals would love to talk to you about the benefits we can provide here at NRG.
